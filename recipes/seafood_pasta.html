Seafood pasta

Description

Seafood Pasta is a quintessential Italian dish that showcases the bounty of the Mediterranean. This delightful pasta dish combines the richness of seafood with the simplicity of Italian cooking, offering a harmonious blend of flavors that evoke the seaside charm of Italy.

Ingredients

For the Seafood and Pasta:

  • 1 pound (450g) pasta, linguine, spaghetti, or your choice
  • 1 pound (450g) mixed seafood, such as shrimp, scallops, mussels, and/or clams
  • 4 tablespoons extra-virgin olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes, adjust to taste
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1/2 cup seafood broth, or chicken broth if seafood broth is unavailable
  • 1/2 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/4 cup fresh parsley, chopped
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

For the Lemon Garlic Butter Sauce:

  • 4 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Zest and juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste

For Garnish (optional):

  • Fresh parsley, chopped
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Steps to make Seafood Pasta

  1. Prepare the Pasta and Seafood:
    • Cook the pasta in a large pot of salted boiling water according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside, reserving about 1 cup of pasta cooking water.
    • Meanwhile, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and red pepper flakes, saut√©ing until fragrant, about 1 minute.
    • Add the mixed seafood to the skillet and cook until just opaque and cooked through. Remove the seafood from the skillet and set aside.

  2. Make the Lemon Garlic Butter Sauce:
    • In the same skillet, melt the butter over medium heat. Add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute.
    • Stir in the lemon zest and juice, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
    • Return the cooked seafood to the skillet and toss to coat in the lemon garlic butter sauce. Remove from heat.

  3. Combine Pasta and Seafood:
    • Add the cooked pasta to the skillet with the seafood and toss gently to combine, adding reserved pasta cooking water as needed to loosen the sauce and coat the pasta evenly.
    • Stir in the halved cherry tomatoes and chopped parsley. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper if necessary.

  4. Serve:
    • Divide the seafood pasta among serving plates or bowls.
    • Garnish with chopped parsley and grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
    • Serve immediately while hot, accompanied by crusty bread and a glass of white wine.

Tips

  • Seafood Selection: Use a variety of seafood for depth of flavor. Ensure all seafood is cleaned and deveined properly.
  • Pasta Cooking Water: The starchy pasta water helps create a silky sauce when combined with the lemon garlic butter mixture.
  • Variations: Add a touch of cream for a richer sauce, or incorporate spinach or arugula for added freshness.

Enjoy your taste of the Italian coast with this flavorful seafood pasta dish!
